How to Measure Square Footage Using Google Earth:

  1. Open Google Earth in your browser.
  2. Search for your property using the address bar.
  3. Zoom in until your lawn, driveway, or walkway is clearly visible.
  4. Click the ruler icon (Measure tool) on the left menu.
  5. Select "Polygon" to trace around the area you want to measure.
  6. Click along the edges to outline the shape. Double-click or click the first point to finish.
  7. Google Earth will show the area in square feet (sq ft).
  8. Use that number in the quote calculators above to get your estimate.
